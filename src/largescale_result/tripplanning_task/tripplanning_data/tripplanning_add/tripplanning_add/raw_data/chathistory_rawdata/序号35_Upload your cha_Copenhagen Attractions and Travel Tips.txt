And here are some important travel tips for Copenhagen:

1. The type of power sockets used in Denmark are Type A and Type K. So, you might need to bring an adapter.

2. The official currency in Denmark is the Danish Krone (DKK). Most places do not accept euros.

3. Copenhagen has 6 metro lines which can take you anywhere in the city.

4. The weather in Copenhagen in December can go below -15 degrees Celsius. So, pack your warmest clothes if you're visiting in winter.

5. The official language is Danish, but English is widely spoken in Copenhagen.

6. Remember, the “hello” in Danish is “hej”, “thank you” is “farvel”, and “goodbye” is “tak”.
